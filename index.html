What will you learn ?

Introduction

Let's start?! And this is exactly where we're going to kick off. In this step, you will learn where to download, how to install the software, plugins that we will use, configure... In addition, you will understand how to create a project within Unreal, import projects in 3dsmax, in Revit, Sketchup, exploring its interface, navigation commands, file structure and much more!!! In this module, I will show you how easy, intuitive and amazing it is to use Unreal.

Lighting

âThe light is the wayâ! Mastering the art of creating, configuring and adjusting lighting correctly in our 3D scenes is crucial. Adjust natural light, artificial light, bounce... Let's learn together how to master Lumen, which developed by Epic Games and present in Unreal Engine, this technology is revolutionizing how we do 3d for architecture, Lumen will surprise you with incredible results, realistic and in real time, without rendering! You know the coolest? I'll show you how to make and master this technology!

Materials

Discover the secret of realistic rendering in Unreal! Learn to represent real-life textures in real-time, without rendering, with incredibly realistic results. Master crafting materials from scratch and take advantage of using Quixel in Unreal Engine for free. Elevate the visual quality of your designs with this powerful combination of Quixel tools and libraries.

Indoor and outdoor scene

Unlock the secrets of spectacular lighting in the iconic Piano House! This unique location offers a perfect opportunity to learn, with its indoor and outdoor areas. Learn how to light and configure the light in a scene that encompasses both outdoors with natural light and indoors with artificial lights, all in a single project. Be prepared to create a magnificent, enveloping atmosphere in any project, regardless of the light and ambience.

Animations

Get ready for an amazing and disruptive Unreal Engine experience! We go beyond static renderings and enter the world of animations. Explore camera movements, transitions, moving elements, light effects and much more. Find out for yourself why Unreal is considered a revolutionary and innovative tool. You'll be able to create complete animations in a matter of minutes, experiencing all the magic and impact they can bring. And best of all: all in real-time, no rendering!

Optimization

Maximize your efficiency and productivity in the competitive market. Don't let your scenes become sluggish, clunky, or consume valuable rendering time, risking losing hours of work due to unexpected crashes. Find out how to optimize your scenes for the best possible performance, without compromising the quality of your work. Learn to be agile when using techniques such as LODS, Nanite and texture optimization, ensuring fast and stable renderings. So you can focus on what really matters: creating and delivering impressive results.

Virtual Tour

Transport your customers into immersive, interactive environments where they can control lighting, change furniture and cladding, all in real time! In the Unreal Engine for Archviz course, you will learn to create realistic and engaging Interactive Virtual Tours. Delight your customers, boost your profits and stand out in the market as a reference in Virtual Tours. We'll teach you how to create interactive menus, change skins, control lights, change colors, open doors, interact with objects and much more!

Car Studio/Products

Welcome to the exciting "Car Studio" module of our course! Here, you'll learn how to create realistic materials for cars and render them with PathTracer technology. Prepare to become an expert in creating high-quality automotive studios and products. Join us on this exciting journey!
